About Spray Money

Spray money is a recording artist from El Centro California. Moving to San Diego in 2007 Spray Money continued his journey in the music scene performing in many different venues all over the city networking and recording with many different artists from different backgrounds of music. Releasing many projects in the last decade including his album The Overpass, Spray Money intends to work on new projects and also schedule future touring dates around the West Coast. Spray Money is currently working on a project with artist Tranzformer and Grey Era on the upcoming LP titled Minority Report. Spray Money has recently been hosting in public speaking event around the city, which include food festivals, personal growth events, and musical competition events.
